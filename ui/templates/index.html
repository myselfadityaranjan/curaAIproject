About This AI Model

This AI model has been trained to identify and predict various types of brain tumors from MRI and CT scan images. It was developed by Aditya Ranjan, with the goal of someday assisting medical professionals in the early detection and diagnosis of brain gliomas and other related tumors.

Currently, the model has achieved significant accuracy — an average accuracy of 82.3% — but it is continuously being improved upon its performance through more training and fine-tuning.

Current issues: The model is correctly trained, however, the "predicted labels" are actually names of subdirectories on which the model was trained. It is currently in the process of being manually updated and fixed.
